Technology Advancements to look out for in 2023 - 24

A look at some promising technology advancements that are set to impact the digital payment landscape.

Open Banking and Digital Wallet Integration:

One exciting development is the integration of open banking APIs with digital wallets, allowing secure connections between wallets and users' bank accounts. This innovation facilitates seamless transactions, real-time fund transfers, and a comprehensive view of financial information, empowering users with personalized recommendations.

Biometric Authentication for Mobile Payments:

Addressing security concerns, mobile payments are embracing biometric authentication methods like fingerprint scanning, facial recognition, and iris scanning. As these technologies evolve, they have the potential to replace traditional password-based authentication, offering enhanced security and a more seamless user experience.

Edge Computing for Private 5G Networks:

The fusion of private 5G networks and edge computing is revolutionizing data processing at the network edge. By bringing computing power closer to data sources, edge computing reduces latency and enables real-time decision-making. Industries such as autonomous vehicles, remote healthcare, and smart manufacturing benefit from improved efficiency and new possibilities for IoT applications through local data processing and analysis.
